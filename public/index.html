About Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh, nestled in the western Himalayas, is one of India's most scenic states. From the snow-capped peaks of Spiti to the lush valleys of Kullu-Manali, it offers something for every traveller — trekking, skiing, paragliding, temple visits, and more.
